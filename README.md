### 🌟 Spotlight Project

#### [🧠 Personal Omniscience Engine](https://github.com/ensibey/personal-omniscience-engine)
*Your data, your language. A personalized local knowledge assistant that doesn't require the internet.*

<p align="center">
  <img src="omniscience_engine.jpg" width="220" alt="Personal Omniscience Engine Logo" style="border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 4px 15px rgba(0,0,0,0.5);" />
</p>

* **Privacy-First:** Fully local architecture powered by LlamaIndex, Qdrant, and Ollama to keep your data secure on your own machine.
* **Auto-Index:** Automatically tracks folder directories to index new documents (PDF, DOCX, MD, TXT) into the vector database.
* **Style Mimicry:** Analyzes writing patterns from your personal notes to generate responses mimicking your unique writing style.
